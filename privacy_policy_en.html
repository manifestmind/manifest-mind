Privacy Policy

Your data belongs to you

Last updated: April 2026
This policy explains how ManifestMind collects, uses and protects your personal data. We are committed to respecting your privacy and handling your data with the utmost care.
01

Who are we?

ManifestMind is a mobile wellness and personal development application. For any privacy-related questions, you can contact us at the address shown at the bottom of this page.

02

Data we collect

We collect only the data strictly necessary for the application to function:

  • Account data: email address, display name (via Google, Apple or email sign-in)
  • First name: entered during initial setup
  • Progress data: completed days, points, unlocked badges, streaks
  • Journal content: text written in the journal section of the app
  • Preferences: chosen language, notification time, app settings
  • Vision Board photos: images you choose to add — stored locally on your device only
We do not collect location data, contacts, biometric data or any information not necessary for the app.
03

How we use your data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate you and secure your account
  • Save and synchronise your progress
  • Personalise your experience (first name, language, daily content)
  • Send local notifications if you have enabled them
  • Improve the application in an anonymous and aggregated way

We never sell your data to third parties. We never use it for advertising purposes.

04

Storage and security

Your data is stored in two ways:

  • Locally on your device via AsyncStorage (progress, journal, vision board) — it does not leave your phone unless you are signed in
  • On our secure servers (Firebase / Google Cloud) for cross-device synchronisation, if you are authenticated

All transmissions are encrypted via HTTPS. We apply industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

05

Sign-in via third-party services

If you choose to sign in via Google or Apple, we receive only the information these services share with us: your email address and display name. We do not have access to your passwords or any other data from your Google or Apple account.

The privacy policies of these services also apply to their own data processing.

06

Notifications

If you enable notifications, they are generated entirely locally on your device. No personal data is transmitted to an external server for sending notifications. You can disable notifications at any time in the application settings or your device settings.

07

Your rights

In accordance with applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights:

  • Access: obtain a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion: delete your account and all your data from the app Settings
  • Portability: receive your data in a readable format
  • Objection: object to certain processing activities

To exercise these rights, use the "Delete my account" function in Settings, or contact us directly.

08

Children's data

ManifestMind is not intended for persons under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

09

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. In the event of a significant change, we will notify you via an in-app notification or by email. The date of the last update is always shown at the top of this page.

Your continued use of the application after any modification constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

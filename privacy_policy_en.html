Privacy Policy

Your data belongs to you

Last updated: April 2026
This policy explains how ManifestMind collects, uses and protects your personal data. We are committed to respecting your privacy and handling your data with the utmost care.
01

Who are we?

ManifestMind is a mobile wellness and personal development application based on a structured journey of 365 thematic cycles. For any privacy-related questions, you can contact us at the address provided at the bottom of this page.

02

Data we collect

We only collect data strictly necessary for the application to function:

  • Account data: email address, display name (via Google, Apple or Magic Link sign-in)
  • First name: entered during initial setup, editable from your Profile
  • Profile photo: chosen from your gallery — stored only on your device
  • Progress data: completed cycles, accumulated points, level reached (Awakened, Blossoming, Radiant, Manifesting)
  • Journal content: texts written in the journal section — stored only on your device
  • Vision Board photos: images chosen from your gallery — stored only on your device
  • Preferences: chosen language (FR/EN/ES), notification time, app settings
We do not collect location data, contacts, biometric data or any information not necessary for the app to function.
03

How we use your data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate you and secure your account
  • Save your progress and cycles
  • Personalize your experience (first name, language, current cycle affirmation)
  • Send local notifications if you have enabled them: your cycle affirmation every morning at the time you choose, and a reminder if your cycle is incomplete at 8pm
  • Improve the application in an anonymous and aggregated way

We never sell your data to third parties. We never use it for advertising purposes.

04

Storage and security

Your data is stored in two ways:

  • Locally on your device only: progress, points, cycles, journal entries, Vision Board photos, profile photo — this data never leaves your phone
  • On our secure servers (Firebase / Google Cloud): only account data (email, display name) for authentication if you are signed in

All transmissions are encrypted via HTTPS. We apply industry standard security measures to protect your data.

05

Sign-in via third-party services

If you choose to sign in via Google or Apple, we only receive the information these services transmit to us: your email address and display name. We do not have access to your passwords or other data from your Google or Apple account.

The privacy policies of these services also apply to their own data processing.

06

Notifications

If you enable notifications, two types are available:

  • Cycle affirmation: sent every morning at the time you choose, it contains the affirmation for your current cycle to accompany you through your day
  • Incomplete cycle reminder: sent at 8pm only if your cycle has not yet been completed that day

These notifications are generated entirely locally on your device. No personal data is transmitted to an external server for their delivery. You can enable or disable each type of notification independently from the app Settings or your device settings.

07

Deletion and reset of your data

You have full control over your data directly from the application:

  • Journey reset: accessible from your Profile. Resets your journey to Cycle 1 and permanently deletes all your points, completed cycles, journal entries and photos. Your account and subscription remain active.
  • Account deletion: accessible from Settings. Permanently deletes your account and all your data. This action is irreversible.
Both actions are irreversible. No data recovery is possible after confirmation.
08

Your rights

In accordance with applicable laws, you have the following rights:

  • Access: obtain a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: correct inaccurate data (first name editable from Profile)
  • Deletion: delete your account and all your data from the app Settings
  • Portability: receive your data in a readable format
  • Objection: object to certain processing

To exercise these rights, use the functions available in the app (Profile and Settings), or contact us directly.

09

Children's data

ManifestMind is not intended for persons under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

10

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. In the event of a significant change, we will notify you via an in-app notification or by email. The date of the last update is always shown at the top of this page.

Continued use of the application after modification constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
